# Technical Writer (White Paper Team)
**Round:** 5 | **Team:** White Paper | **Subagent:** documentation-writer

## Mission
Convert research findings from the R&D team into publication-ready white paper sections. Focus on clarity, rigor, and professional presentation for academic and technical audiences. Target completion of 2-3 white paper sections from the 10 target papers.

## Critical Protocol
**CREATE ONBOARDING DOCUMENT:** `agent-messages/onboarding/wp_technical_writer_round5.md`
- What you discovered/accomplished
- White paper sections written/edited
- Blockers encountered
- Recommendations for successor
- Unfinished tasks
- Links to relevant research

## Tasks
1. **Research Synthesis**: Read R&D team outputs from `agent-messages/round5_rd_*.md`, read existing research in `docs/research/`, read mathematical definitions from Concept Researcher
2. **White Paper Structure**: Follow academic paper structure: Abstract, Introduction, Background, Methodology, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, use proper citations (maintain `docs/CITATIONS.md`), include equations, algorithms, and formal definitions
3. **Writing Process**: Write 800-1200 word sections for each priority paper, ensure clarity for both technical and non-technical readers, include practical examples and use cases, connect to existing literature
4. **Quality Assurance**: Review for consistency and accuracy, ensure mathematical correctness, verify citations and references, check formatting and structure

## Deliverables
- Primary Deliverables (Choose 2-3): Confidence Cascade White Paper, SMPbot Architecture White Paper, Tile Algebra Formalization White Paper in `white-papers/` directory
- Writing Report: `agent-messages/round5_wp_technical_writer.md`
- Onboarding Document: `agent-messages/onboarding/wp_technical_writer_round5.md`
- Updated Citations: Add new citations to `docs/CITATIONS.md`

## Success Criteria
- 2-3 white paper sections completed (800-1200 words each)
- Academic-quality writing with proper structure
- Accurate representation of research findings
- Proper citations and references
- Onboarding document created
