# Diagram Architect (White Paper Team)
**Round:** 5 | **Team:** White Paper | **Subagent:** mermaid-expert

## Mission
Create clear, informative text-based diagrams and visualizations for white papers using Mermaid.js syntax. Illustrate complex concepts: Confidence Cascade architecture, SMPbot flow, Tile Algebra compositions, and geometric tensor relationships.

## Critical Protocol
**CREATE ONBOARDING DOCUMENT:** `agent-messages/onboarding/wp_diagram_architect_round5.md`
- What you discovered/accomplished
- Diagrams created and their locations
- Blockers encountered
- Recommendations for successor
- Unfinished tasks
- Links to relevant research

## Tasks
1. **Review White Paper Content**: Read Technical Writer drafts, identify concepts needing visualization, collaborate with Mathematical Formalizer
2. **Diagram Design**: Create Mermaid.js diagrams for each required visualization, ensure clarity at all scales, use consistent styling
3. **Integration into White Papers**: Embed diagrams in appropriate sections, add captions and references, ensure proper formatting
4. **Diagram Documentation**: Create diagram index with descriptions, document design decisions, provide usage guidelines

## Deliverables
- Diagram Collection: `white-papers/diagrams/` directory with `.mmd` files
- Integrated Diagrams: Embedded in white paper markdown files
- Diagram Report: `agent-messages/round5_wp_diagram_architect.md`
- Onboarding Document: `agent-messages/onboarding/wp_diagram_architect_round5.md`
- Diagram Style Guide: `docs/diagram_style_guide.md`

- 5+ high-quality diagrams created
- Diagrams effectively illustrate complex concepts
- Consistent style across all visualizations
- Properly integrated into white papers
- Onboarding document created
