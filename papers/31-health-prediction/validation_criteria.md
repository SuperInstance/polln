## Claims to Validate

### Primary Claims
1. **Health metrics predict system failures** (r > 0.7 correlation)
2. **Proactive intervention prevents >80% of failures**
3. **Prediction precision >70%** (true positive rate)
4. **Multi-dimensional metrics outperform single metrics**

### Secondary Claims
1. **Anomaly detection enables early warning**
2. **Health score correlates with time to failure**
3. **Resource utilization metrics are predictive**
4. **Error rate is leading indicator of failure**
