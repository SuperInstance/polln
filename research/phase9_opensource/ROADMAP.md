## Vision

SuperInstance aims to become the standard framework for:
- **Distributed Intelligence**: Building systems that think at scale
- **Swarm Intelligence**: Coordinating agent networks effectively
- **Adaptive Computation**: Systems that optimize themselves
- **Research Platform**: Advancing understanding of collective intelligence

## Planning Process

### Roadmap Updates

The roadmap is updated quarterly through:
1. Community feedback and proposals
2. Technical needs and discoveries
3. Resource availability
4. Priority assessments

### Priority Criteria

Features and improvements are prioritized based on:
- **Community Demand**: Number of requests, votes, discussions
- **Technical Debt**: Impact on maintainability and performance
- **Research Value**: Novel contributions to the field
- **Strategic Alignment**: Alignment with project vision
- **Resource Requirements**: Effort vs. impact

### Emerging Opportunities

The roadmap remains flexible for:
- Unexpected research breakthroughs
- Community-driven initiatives
- Partnership opportunities
- Technological advances
- Market demands
