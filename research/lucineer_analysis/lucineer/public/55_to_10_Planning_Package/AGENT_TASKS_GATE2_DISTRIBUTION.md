# Agent Task Breakdown: Gate 2 - Distribution Strategy

## Overview

**Objective:** Validate distribution channels and secure partner agreements
**Timeline:** Days 60-90
**Success Criteria:** 3+ partner negotiations, 1+ signed agreement, GTM playbook
**Current Score Impact:** 6.5 → 7.0 (+0.5)

## Distribution Channel Analysis

### Channel Options

| Channel | Margin | Volume | CAC | Speed | Priority |
|---------|--------|--------|-----|-------|----------|
| Direct Sales | 100% | Low | High | Slow | 🟡 Build initially |
| Distributors | 70% | Medium | Medium | Medium | 🔴 Primary focus |
| Online (Digikey/Mouser) | 75% | High | Low | Fast | 🟠 Secondary |
| OEM Design-In | 85% | High | High | Slow | 🟡 Long-term |

### Recommended Channel Mix

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6):**
- 70% Distributor
- 20% Direct (strategic accounts)
- 10% Online

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12):**
- 50% Distributor
- 30% Direct
- 20% Online

**Phase 3 (Months 13+):**
- 30% Distributor
- 40% Direct
- 30% Online

## Task Breakdown by Agent

### Agent 1: Distribution Research Agent

#### Task 1.1: Distributor Landscape Analysis
**Priority:** 🔴 CRITICAL
**Duration:** 3 days
**Dependencies:** None

**Description:**
Map the electronics distributor landscape for edge AI chips.

**Steps:**
1. Identify major distributors (Arrow, Avnet, Digikey, Mouser, etc.)
2. Research their edge AI portfolios
3. Understand their customer bases
4. Analyze their typical deal structures
5. Identify gaps and opportunities

**Research Targets:**
| Distributor | Edge AI Focus | Customer Base | Typical Margin |
|-------------|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| Arrow | Medium | Enterprise | 25-30% |
| Avnet | High | Industrial | 25-30% |
| Digikey | High | Makers/Pro | 15-20% |
| Mouser | Medium | Makers/Pro | 15-20% |
| Future | Medium | Enterprise | 25-30% |
| Rochester | Low | Industrial | 25-30% |

**Outputs:**
- Distributor comparison matrix
- Recommended partners (ranked)
- Deal structure benchmarks
- Gap analysis

---

#### Task 1.2: Distribution Partner Selection
**Priority:** 🔴 CRITICAL
**Duration:** 2 days
**Dependencies:** Task 1.1

**Description:**
Select top distribution partners to pursue.

**Selection Criteria:**
1. Edge AI expertise
2. Relevant customer base
3. Technical support capability
4. Geographic coverage
5. Willingness to promote new products

**Steps:**
1. Score each distributor
2. Weight criteria by importance
3. Rank candidates
4. Select top 3-5 for outreach
5. Document rationale

**Outputs:**
- Scored distributor list
- Top 5 target partners
- Outreach priority order
- Initial contact strategy

### Agent 3: Negotiation Agent

#### Task 3.1: Term Sheet Development
**Priority:** 🟠 HIGH
**Duration:** 3 days
**Dependencies:** Task 2.2

**Description:**
Develop term sheet for distribution partnership.

**Key Terms to Define:**
| Term | Target | Acceptable Range |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Distributor margin | 25% | 20-30% |
| Payment terms | Net 30 | Net 30-60 |
| Exclusivity | None | Geographic limited |
| Minimum commitment | $100K/year | $50-200K |
| Marketing support | Co-op | 50-100% co-op |
| Technical training | Provided | Free training |

**Steps:**
1. Define target terms
2. Research market benchmarks
3. Identify negotiable items
4. Create term sheet template
5. Legal review

**Outputs:**
- Term sheet template
- Negotiation parameters
- Red lines defined
- Legal approval

---

#### Task 3.2: Partner Negotiation
**Priority:** 🔴 CRITICAL
**Duration:** 7 days
**Dependencies:** Task 3.1

**Description:**
Negotiate partnership terms with priority partner.

**Inputs:**
- Priority partner feedback
- Term sheet template
- Negotiation parameters

**Steps:**
1. Present initial terms
2. Address partner counterproposals
3. Negotiate key terms
4. Reach agreement
5. Draft agreement

**Negotiation Strategy:**
- Lead with business value
- Be flexible on non-critical terms
- Protect pricing control
- Ensure performance requirements
- Build in review periods

**Outputs:**
- Negotiated terms
- Draft distribution agreement
- Partner commitment
- Implementation timeline

---

#### Task 3.3: Agreement Execution
**Priority:** 🔴 CRITICAL
**Duration:** 3 days
**Dependencies:** Task 3.2

**Steps:**
