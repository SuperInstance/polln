## 1. WHAT KIMI IS UNIQUELY GOOD AT

Based on Kimi's architecture and capabilities, here are the research tasks where it provides maximum value:

**Kimi's Advantage**: 200K+ token context window can process entire research papers, legal documents, and technical specifications in a single read.

**Best Use**:

### 1.2 Real-Time Web Browsing
**Kimi's Advantage**: Can browse the current web, not just training data.

**Best Use**:
- Competitive intelligence (Taalas, Hailo announcements)

**Why This Complements Me**: My knowledge has a cutoff. Kimi can check what happened TODAY and provide URLs.

### 1.3 Native Asian Language Research
**Kimi's Advantage**: Exceptional Chinese language understanding, good Japanese/Korean capabilities.

**Best Use**:
- CNKI (知网) searches for Chinese academic papers
- Japanese NEDO project documentation
- Korean KAIST research pages
- Chinese startup announcements (Baidu, Alibaba, Huawei ecosystems)

**Why This Complements Me**: I can translate, but Kimi understands cultural context and can search native-language sources that don't appear in English search results.

### 1.4 Multi-Agent Parallelization
**Kimi's Advantage**: Swarm agents can tackle independent tasks simultaneously.

**Best Use**:
- 7 different research areas in parallel
- Cross-verification across sources
- Competitive + technical + investor research at once

**Why This Complements Me**: I can orchestrate, but parallel execution by specialized agents is faster and more thorough.

## 4. BANG FOR THE BUCK — PRIORITIZED TASKS

If budget is limited, prioritize these Kimi tasks in order:

### Priority 1: Chinese Language Research (HIGHEST VALUE)
**Why**: Chinese AI chip development is moving fast, and English sources miss 50%+ of what's happening. BitNet alternatives, 2T1C competitors, and memory supply chain intelligence are all in Chinese sources.

**Specific Ask**: Native CNKI searches for edge AI chips, ternary inference, and LLM accelerators.

### Priority 2: Real-Time Competitive Intelligence (HIGH VALUE)
**Why**: The Taalas situation is fluid. Their edge pivot probability changes weekly. Job postings and investor updates provide leading indicators.

**Specific Ask**: Search Taalas for "edge", "mobile", "low-power" in real-time. Monitor Hailo announcements.

### Priority 3: Paper Deep-Reads (HIGH VALUE)
**Why**: The technical papers (2T1C, Fairy ±i, BitNet) have hardware implications buried in methodology sections. Kimi can extract exact specifications.

**Specific Ask**: Read papers and extract: cell sizes, power consumption, process compatibility, performance metrics.

### Priority 4: Customer Sentiment Analysis (MEDIUM VALUE)
**Why**: Reddit and forum discussions reveal what users ACTUALLY think, not what marketing claims.

**Specific Ask**: Extract specific complaints about Hailo LLM performance with direct quotes.

### Priority 5: Investor Intelligence (MEDIUM VALUE)
**Why**: Knowing WHICH VCs invest in edge AI saves time.

**Specific Ask**: Cross-reference edge AI investments with seed-stage check sizes.
