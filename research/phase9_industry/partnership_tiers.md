# SuperInstance Industry Partnership Tiers

## Overview

SuperInstance offers three partnership tiers designed to accommodate organizations ranging from academic research labs to Fortune 500 enterprises. Each tier provides increasing levels of support, features, and customization options.

## Tier 1: Research Partner

### Target Audience
- Academic research institutions
- Individual researchers and graduate students
- Open-source contributors
- Non-profit organizations

### Benefits
- Full access to SuperInstance open-source codebase
- Community support via GitHub Issues and Discord
- Quarterly research webinars
- Citation support and co-authorship opportunities
- Free usage for non-commercial research

### Limitations
- No guaranteed support response times
- No custom feature development
- No SLA guarantees
- Commercial usage requires paid tier

### Onboarding Process
1. Sign up on GitHub
2. Join Discord community
3. Review documentation
4. Start using SuperInstance

### Cost
**Free** - Community supported

## Upgrade Paths

### Research → Strategic
- Trigger: Commercial usage or need for support
- Process: 30-day transition period
- Incentive: First month free
- Migration assistance included

### Strategic → Enterprise
- Trigger: Scale or compliance requirements
- Process: 60-day transition period
- Incentive: 20% discount first year
- Custom feature migration included

### Downgrade Options
- All tiers can downgrade with 90-day notice
- Custom features remain available
- Support adjusts immediately
- No pro-rated refunds

## Special Programs

### Startup Accelerator Program
- Eligibility: <50 employees, <$5M raised
- Benefits: Strategic tier for $5K/month for first year
- Application: Pitch deck + technical assessment
- Limit: 10 companies per cohort

- Eligibility: 5+ merged PRs to SuperInstance
- Benefits: 50% discount on Strategic tier
- Application: Automated based on contribution history
- Limit: Unlimited

### Research Grant Program
- Eligibility: Academic researchers with funding
- Benefits: Free Strategic tier for grant duration
- Application: Grant proposal + letter of support
- Limit: 20 grants per year

### Social Impact Program
- Eligibility: Non-profits, NGOs, B-Corps
- Benefits: Discounted Strategic tier ($5K/month)
- Application: Organization documentation
- Limit: 15 organizations per year
