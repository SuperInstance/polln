## The Breakthrough Test

Before documenting anything, ask:

**Is this a breakthrough?**

- вқҢ Faster version of existing thing
- вқҢ Someone else already wrote about this
- вң… **Fundamentally new capability**
- вң… **Enables impossible workflows**
- вң… **Changes how we think about AI**

If it's not a breakthrough, don't document it. We only want the big ideas.

## Writing Guidelines

### Voice

```
вқҢ BAD: "The system implements a novel architecture for..."
вң… GOOD: "Here's the thing: we broke the AI into pieces."

вқҢ BAD: "Utilization of the aforementioned methodology..."
вң… GOOD: "Use this and watch what happens."
```

### Structure

1. **Hook** - Why should they care?
2. **Idea** - What is it?
3. **Breakthrough** - What can you do now?
4. **How** - Under the hood
5. **Example** - Show it working
6. **Science** - Why it works
7. **Future** - Where it's going
