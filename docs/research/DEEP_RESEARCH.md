# Deep Research & Simulations - Round 2

**Objective**: Spawn deep research agents to explore theoretical foundations and run simulations to validate POLLN's architecture.

## Research Themes

1. **Quantum-Inspired Agent Coordination**
   - Explore quantum entanglement as metaphor for agent communication
   - Superposition of agent states
   - Measurement and collapse in decision making

2. **Neuroscientific Plausibility**
   - Compare POLLN architecture to actual brain circuits
   - Hebbian learning validation
   - Subsumption architecture in biological systems

3. **Thermodynamic Efficiency**
   - Agent colonies as energy systems
   - Entropy and information processing
   - Free energy principle applications

4. **Game-Theoretic Analysis**
   - Agent incentives and mechanism design
   - Cooperative vs competitive equilibria
   - Reputation and trust systems

5. **Simulation & Validation**
   - Agent performance under stress
   - Scaling laws and phase transitions
   - Emergent behavior discovery

Each agent will produce both theoretical research AND executable simulations.
